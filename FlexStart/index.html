COVID-19 spreads mainly from person to person through respiratory droplets. Thus, the purpose of our software is to discourage ignorance of not wearing masks, using face mask detection


The key benefits of this software is that it sends constant reminders to people not wearing masks and thus encouraging them to be safe. Everyone in the institution shall benefit from the software.
